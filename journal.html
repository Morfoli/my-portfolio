How I improved my writing

October 2024

At the start of the year, I struggled with conclusions — I would just repeat my introduction. My teacher suggested I think of the conclusion as answering "So what?" Why does this matter? I started ending my essays by connecting my argument to a bigger idea or asking a question for the reader to consider. My last two essays got much better feedback on the conclusion section. Small change, big impact.
